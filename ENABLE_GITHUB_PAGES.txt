HOW TO ENABLE GITHUB PAGES - STEP BY STEP
===========================================

YOUR SITE: https://johnkibre.github.io

PROBLEM: Site works on PC but not on phone
REASON: GitHub Pages is not enabled yet
SOLUTION: Follow these steps:

STEP 1: Open this link on your PC
----------------------------------------
https://github.com/johnkibre/johnkibre.github.io/settings/pages

(You may need to log in to GitHub first)


STEP 2: Configure GitHub Pages
----------------------------------------
You will see a page titled "GitHub Pages"

Look for "Build and deployment" section

Under "Source":
  - Click the dropdown
  - Select: "Deploy from a branch"

Under "Branch":
  - First dropdown: Select "main"
  - Second dropdown: Select "/ (root)"
  - Click the green "Save" button


STEP 3: Wait for Deployment
----------------------------------------
After clicking Save, you'll see:
"GitHub Pages source saved"

Wait 2-3 minutes for the site to build.

Refresh the page and you'll see:
âœ… "Your site is live at https://johnkibre.github.io"


STEP 4: Test on Your Phone
----------------------------------------
Open your phone browser and go to:
https://johnkibre.github.io

You should see your colorful 3D portfolio! ðŸŽ‰


TROUBLESHOOTING:
================

If you see 404 error:
- Wait 5 more minutes
- Make sure you selected "main" branch
- Make sure you clicked "Save"

If styles don't load:
- Clear your phone browser cache
- Try incognito/private mode

If it still doesn't work:
- Check: https://github.com/johnkibre/johnkibre.github.io/actions
- Look for green checkmark (means deployment succeeded)
